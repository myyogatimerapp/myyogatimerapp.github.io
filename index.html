What's this all about?

MyYoga Timer allows you to time your yoga routine with ease without clumsily trying to reset a timer each time you change pose. It gives you the flexibility of using minimal cues to move to your next pose, without the hassle of trying to follow along with a yoga video at home. If you like the freedom of doing yoga on your own but still want some structure to your routine, MyYoga Timer has got you covered.

Adding poses to your custom routine

Time Your Yoga Practice

One of the biggest obstacles of doing yoga own your own, is holding your pose for long enough. MyYoga Timer allows you to easily time your pose, giving you multiple cues when to move to the next pose.

Build your own routines

Build your own routines easily by combining poses together that work best for you. You can copy premade rotines and edit them as you like, or create a totally new routine.

Track Your Progress

Track your progress to see when you did & didn't practice your yoga.

By allowing you to create small routines, you can build a habit of doing some yoga each day, no matter how much time you have available, and track it as you go.
